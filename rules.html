Rules

In general, don't be a dick. If you're doing something that's upsetting other people, clearly we're going to have a problem.

  • Respect fellow members of the computer science discord. Just don't be a cunt to someone.
  • No slurs of any kind allowed, including but not limited to (Ableist, Transphobic, Homophobic,Racist,Anti-Semitic etc) if you are unsure if you shouldn't say a word, just don't say it.
  • Please use each channel for its intended purpose. If there is a channel to post something, post it there.
  • Please respect UEA's policies on plagiarism and collusion. This includes aiding and asking for help for assessments which are intended to be independent. Any breach of such will reflect badly on the whole discord.
  • Please keep in line with the Discords Terms of Service
  • Please only ping admins for a reason. Ping @Very Depressed Owner before pinging admins with an issue
  • Do not flood/spam the channels. 6+ Messages count as flooding.
  • Do not try to loophole/bypass punishments or rules.
  • Images/Videos/Gifs etc containing slurs will incur the same punishment as Rule no.2.
